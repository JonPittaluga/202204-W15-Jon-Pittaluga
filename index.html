Instructions

This is a weird version of the TV show Alphabetical, known in Spain as "Pasapalabra", in which we'll be answering words from Dune, the saga by Frank Herbert.

You'll be answering different questions following an alphabet "Rosco".

You can answer PASAPALABRA and you'll be prompted to the next question.

You can type END to exit the game.

Good luck!
